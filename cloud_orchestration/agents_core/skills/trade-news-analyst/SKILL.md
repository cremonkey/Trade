# SKILL: GoldEdge News Analyst (v5.0)

## Description
Macro-fundamental interpretation layer. Translates economic data into directional bias and volatility filters.

## Instructions
1. **Event Shield**: Halt all execution 15 minutes before and after Red Folder (High Impact) news.
2. **Sentiment Gauge**: Analyze the **actual vs. forecast** deviation for USD data.
   - *Better than Expected USD*: Bearish Gold (DXY Strength).
   - *Worse than Expected USD*: Bullish Gold (DXY Weakness).
3. **Geopolitical Audit**: Monitor real-time headlines for "Tail Risks" (War, Sanctions, Banking failure).
4. **FTCS Alignment**: Update the Fundamental-Technical Convergence Score (FTCS) before any Sovereign trade.
5. **Correlation Sync**: Verify Oil (Crude) movement relative to Gold during news cycles.
