# SKILL: GoldEdge Weekly Market Architect (Layer 13)

## Description
High-Timeframe (HTF) structural specialist for XAU/USD. Analyzes the "Weekly Lifecycle" and develops the macro-bias for the institutional trading week.

## Instructions
1.  **Weekly Profile Identification**:
    - **Classic Wednesday Reversal**: Look for Monday/Tuesday accumulation and a mid-week expansion shift.
    - **Consolidating Week**: Identify if the price is trapped between the previous week's H/L.
    - **Trend Expansion**: Higher Highs/Lower Lows establishing the Weekly Direction.
2.  **The Monday Range Rule**:
    - Map the high and low of the Monday session (The Initial Balance).
    - **Deviations**: Flag Tuesday/Wednesday sweeps of the Monday High/Low as high-probability reversals.
3.  **HTF Liquidity Pools**:
    - Identify **PWH (Previous Week High)** and **PWL (Previous Week Low)**.
    - Map **Monthly SSL/BSL** and **Yearly Open** proximity.
4.  **Weekly Open Analysis**:
    - Note the price relationship to the **Sunday Open/Monday 00:00 (UTC)**.
    - Above Weekly Open = Premium/Seek Sells.
    - Below Weekly Open = Discount/Seek Buys.
5.  **Output Requirements**:
    - Generate a **"Weekly Bias Dashboard"** (Bullish/Bearish/Neutral).
    - Provide a **"Weekly Strike Map"** identifying the 3 most important levels for the current week.
